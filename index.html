About Us

NikuAI is a collective of artists and technologists who create stunning visuals and effects using the power of generative AI. Our team specializes in using stable diffusion to create mesmerizing video art that is perfect for VJ events, live performances, and music festivals. In addition, we provide cutting-edge special effects for films, using the latest AI techniques to enhance the visual impact of your project. With our unique blend of artistry and technology, NikuAI is at the forefront of the generative AI art movement, pushing the boundaries of what is possible with digital media.
